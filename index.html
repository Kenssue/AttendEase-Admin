Welcome to AttendEase

Your seamless solution for QR Code-based attendance tracking and management.

How to Use This Platform:

1. LogIn Process

Open the AttendEase login page, enter your admin password, and click “Generate OTP.” Once the OTP appears, enter it and click “Login” to access the dashboard.

2. Attendance Management

In the Attendance Dashboard, select a section to view records. Search by name, filter by status, sort columns, and click “Refresh Data” for updates.

3. Account Control

Manage student accounts by searching, activating/deactivating, or deleting them (requires a reason). You can also show/hide passwords using the eye icon.

4. Exporting Data

To export, filter the attendance list as needed and click “Export PDF.” The file includes student info, status, timestamp, and auto-generates a filename.

5. Security

The system logs out on inactivity or browser close. OTP login, hidden passwords, and restricted copy/paste enhance security.

6. Troubleshooting

If login fails, verify credentials or clear your browser cache. For data issues, refresh the page or reset filters. Contact the admin for further help.
